His Works

Ibn Taymiyyah was a prolific writer and authored many works spanning a broad range of topics. The sum of his writings were thought to consist of hundreds of volumes and even though a large number of them have been lost, many are still available and in print. A number of his works have also been translated and below is a list of these works followed by some of his works in Arabic.

The books of, or about, ibn Taymiyyah available in the English language:

  • Ibn Taymiyyah on Public and Private Law in Islam or Public Policy in Islamic Jurisprudence
  • A Seventh Century Sunni Creed: The Aqida al-Wasitiya of ibn Taymya
  • Public Duties in Islam
  • Ibn Taymiyyah's Essay on the Jinn
  • The Friends of Allah and the Friends of Shaytan
  • Ibn Taymiyyah Against the Greek Logicians
  • Aqeedah al-Waasitiyyah
  • Diseases of the Hearts and their Cures
  • The Relief from Distress

The available Arabic works of ibn Taymiyyah are many, from amongst them:

  • Majmu` Fatawa ibn Taymiyyah
  • Fatawa al-Kubra
  • Fatawa al-Misriyyah
  • Minhaj as-Sunnah an-Nabawiyyah
  • Naqd at-Ta'sis
  • Al-Istiqamah
  • Tafsir ibn Taymiyyah
