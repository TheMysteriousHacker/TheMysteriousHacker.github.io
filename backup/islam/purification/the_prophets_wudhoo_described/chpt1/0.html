Wudhoo in the Arabic Language

Wudhoo is the action, and wadoo the water used therefore - and it is also a verbal noun; or they are two forms of the same word, both meaning the verbal-noun and maybe both referring also to the water used.

Wudhoo in the Sharee'ah

Wudhoo is using clean and cleansing (Tuhoor) water upon certain body parts as Allaah Ta'aala has prescribed and explained.
